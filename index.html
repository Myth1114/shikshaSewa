Frequently Asked Questions

Our FAQ section is designed to provide clear and concise answers to the most common queries about studying abroad. From choosing the right country and university to visa applications, scholarships, and post-study work opportunities, we cover everything you need to know. Whether you're curious about tuition fees, part-time work, or admission requirements, this section helps you make informed decisions with confidence. If you have additional questions, feel free to contact our expert team for personalized guidance!

1. How do I know which country and university are right for me?

Our expert counselors assess your academic background, career goals, budget, and preferences to help you choose the best country and university that aligns with your aspirations.

2. Can you help with scholarship applications?

Yes, we guide students in applying for scholarships, grants, and financial aid offered by universities and governments to help reduce tuition costs.

3. Do you assist with visa applications?

Yes! Our experts help with the entire visa lodgement process, including document preparation, financial proof guidance, visa interviews, and submission to ensure a higher success rate.

4. Does Shiksha Sewa Pvt. Ltd. charge for counseling?

Our initial counseling is free! We charge a nominal fee for premium services like university applications, visa processing, and test preparation, depending on the package you choose.

5. How do I get started with Shiksha Sewa Pvt. Ltd.?

You can visit our office, call us, or contact us through our website or social media to book a free counseling session. Our team will guide you through the entire process from application to visa approval.
