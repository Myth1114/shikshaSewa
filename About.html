ABOUT US

Welcome to Shiksha Sewa

Shiksha Sewa Pvt. Ltd. is a leading education consultancy based in Nepal, dedicated to guiding students toward achieving their academic aspirations abroad. Established with the mission of delivering transparent, reliable, and personalized services, we have become a trusted partner for students exploring global educational opportunities.

Integrity

We prioritize honesty and transparency in every interaction, ensuring that students and their families trust us throughout their journey.

Student-Centric Approach

Our focus is always on the unique needs and goals of each student, providing personalized solutions to help them succeed.

Excellence

We are committed to delivering the highest quality of services, from expert counseling to seamless application processes.

Collaboration

By building strong partnerships with renowned universities and fostering meaningful relationships with students, we create pathways to global academic success.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between students and global education opportunities by providing transparent, ethical, and expert guidance. We aim to empower students with knowledge, confidence, and the right resources to succeed in their academic and professional journeys abroad.

Why Choose us?

  • Globally Recognized Universities
  • Strong STEM & Medicine Programs
  • Scholarships & Government Grants
  • Booming Job Market
  • Cultural & Language Exposure

Our Vision

We envision becoming the most trusted education consultancy in Nepal by helping thousands of students achieve their study-abroad goals with ease. Through our expert assistance, we strive to make global education accessible, affordable, and achievable.
